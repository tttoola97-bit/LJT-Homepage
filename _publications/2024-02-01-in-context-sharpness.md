This research introduces a novel approach to hallucination detection and mitigation in language models by analyzing in-context sharpness from the perspective of inner representations. The method provides alerts when models are likely to generate hallucinated content.

**Authors**: Shiqi Chen, Miao Xiong, Junteng Liu, Zhengxuan Wu, Teng Xiao, Siyang Gao, Junxian He

**Venue**: ICML 2024 (International Conference on Machine Learning)
